About G-TECH Limited

Industrial Expertise, Trusted Solutions

Based in Mukono, Uganda (Mukono Kayunga Road, Block 149, Plot 1105), G-TECH Limited is a premier industrial services provider serving major regional contractors and industrial operators across East Africa. Our team of over 35 professionals—engineers, technicians, quality specialists, and economists—delivers precision-engineered solutions for industrial maintenance, machinery installation, gas manufacturing, civil works, and electromechanical contracting.
